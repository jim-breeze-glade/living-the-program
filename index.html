Am I clean today?

How have I acted differently?

Did my disease run my life today?


How?


What did I do today that I wish I had not done?

What have I left undone that I wish I had done?

Was I good to myself today?


How?

Was today a good day?

Was I happy?

Was I serene?

Just for today, I will have faith in someone in NA who belives in me and wants to help me in my recovery.

Did I talk to my sponsor today?

Did I attend a meeting today?


Where?


Did I share my experiences, strengths, and hopes?

Who are the people in my life that I trust today?

Who has trust in me today?

Just for today, I will have a program. I will try to follow it to the best of my ability.

Did I read from our literature today?

What steps did I consciously work?

Did I admit my powerlessness today?

Was I able to put my trust in my Higher Power today?

What did I learn about myself today?

Did I make any amends today?

Do I owe any?

Did I admit fault to anyone today?

Did I worry about yesterday or tomorrow?

Can I accept myself as I am today?

Just for today, through NA, I will try to get a better perspective on my life.

Did I feel like I was a part of humanity today?

Did I allow myself to become obsessed by anything today?

What has God given me to be grateful for today?

Have I done anything to cause harm to myself or to another today?


What?


Am I willing to change today?

Did I pray or meditate today?

How did this affect my life?

What spiritual principles have I been able to practice in my life today?

Was the most important thing in my life today staying clean?

Notes

I'm Jim, and I'm an addict

Hi, I'm Jim and thanks to the program of Narcotics Anonymous I've been given an opportunity to leave behind a life in active addiction to mind-altering substances and pursue a much more productive addiction to coding and software development. This site is a personal project that accomplishes two things:

1.) Let's me fill out the "Living the Program" IP without all that damn handwriting.
2.) Gives me a project to teach myself full stack web development.

And while this is a personal project, you are free to use it yourself as long as you are aware of the following:

1.) Your data is stored in your browser's 'localstorage' so you can only access your history in the current browser, and that data is NOT encrypted. For now maybe add "just kidding" if you're admitting to some serious crime or something.
2.) I plan on improving this site when I have time between a full-time job and college and all the rest of life, so updates might be plenty some times and few at others. Also if you have any suggestions my email is jimh.sprh@gmail.com, but as welcome as suggestions are, I might not get around to, or even want to implement them, so no hard feelings.

Anyways, I hope you enjoy your day!
